ABOUT THE GAME:

Description:

The game is a puzzle that helps you to have fun and learn at the same time.It is a number puzzle game. You join the numbers until you get to 2048 or a higher score.

How to play?

It's simple to play, but hard to master and very addictive. OBJECTIVE: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one and you get points for that! In this attempt, try to get a block of 2048 and you win the game!
